TIGERS

The Tiger is the largest wild cat in the world. The Tigers tail is three feet long. On average the Tigers weighs 450 pounds.

It stands three feet tall with teeth four inches long and claws as long as house keys.

Common Name: Bengal Tiger

scientific name: Panthera Tigris Tigris

Type: Mammals

Diet: Carnivore

Average Life Span in the Wild: 8 to 10 years

Size: Head and body: 5 to 6 feet; tail: 2 to 3 feet

Weight: 240 to 500 pounds
